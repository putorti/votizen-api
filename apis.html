APIs¶

Basic Resources¶

Our basic APIs allow access to single services or information about specific resources.

Location Services¶

/geocode/
  • input: a CASS-certified postal address
  • output: longitude/latitude coordinates and estimated precision.
/district/
  • input: longitude/latitude coordinates, precision
  • output: electoral district IDs containing these coordinates

Voter Data¶

/voter/
  • input: Name, location, and date of birth
  • output: whether we were able to match the query to a voter roll record and, if matched, the Votizen-assigned Voter ID (for use in other Voter resources).
/voter/record/
  • input: Voter ID
  • output: party registration and voting history for the requested voter

Composite Resources¶

Our composite resources chain together two or more of our Basic Resources in commonly-used combinations. This is convenient, saves you requests, and prevents you from waiting for multiple round-trip requests so you get your answers faster.

/ballot/
/geocode/ -> /district/ -> collection of in-district races and respective candidates
